# Brand Equity

Theoretical skill for measuring and reasoning about brand equity, anchored in Aaker and Keller frameworks.

## When to invoke

- When evaluating the equity impact of a brand decision
- When building a brand-equity measurement framework for a sub-brand
- When a strategic discussion needs grounding in brand-equity theory
