# Brand Equity

The theoretical and operational resource for evaluating and building brand equity across
Every's portfolio. Anchored in Aaker, Keller, Kapferer, and the Ehrenberg-Bass tradition,
this skill provides the shared vocabulary and evaluation methodology for equity-level work.

Loaded by all positioning skills and by brand-voice skills when doing equity-level work —
any task that asks "how strong is this brand?" or "how do we make it stronger?" routes
through here.

## System

Brand equity is composed of five reference files, each covering a distinct theoretical
or operational lens:

### references/aaker.md
David Aaker's brand identity system and brand equity model. The architectural lens —
how brand equity is structured, measured, and managed as an asset.

### references/keller.md
Kevin Lane Keller's Customer-Based Brand Equity (CBBE) pyramid and brand resonance
model. The consumer lens — how equity builds from awareness through to loyalty.

### references/kapferer.md
Jean-Noël Kapferer's brand identity prism. The identity lens — how a brand's internal
and external facets create a coherent identity.

### references/sharp-ehrenberg.md
Byron Sharp and Andrew Ehrenberg's work on distinctive brand assets and mental/physical
availability. The evidence lens — what actually drives brand growth at scale.

### references/evaluation-rubric.md
How we score brand equity in practice at Every. The operational lens — the rubric that
translates theory into evaluable criteria across our portfolio.

## When to invoke

- When evaluating the equity strength of an Every sub-brand
- When a positioning skill needs brand equity context to inform its framework
- When building or auditing a brand-voice for equity alignment
- When diagnosing where a brand is stuck (awareness, associations, loyalty, resonance)
- When making a strategic decision that trades off short-term activation against
  long-term brand building

## Reference routing

| Task type | Load |
|---|---|
| Brand architecture or equity structure | `references/aaker.md` |
| Consumer-side equity diagnosis (salience, resonance) | `references/keller.md` |
| Brand identity coherence or prism analysis | `references/kapferer.md` |
| Distinctiveness, mental availability, growth drivers | `references/sharp-ehrenberg.md` |
| Scoring or evaluating a specific brand's equity | `references/evaluation-rubric.md` |
