# A Positioning Year: H2 2026 Through H1 2027

The market does not yet hold Every's Editorial work, software, training, research, and
Consulting as one clear company. Marketing's job through H1 2027 is to establish one
position:

**Every makes you AI native by pioneering the future of work.**

## Perception shift

| Current impression | Position to establish |
|---|---|
| An AI newsletter | The best way to become AI native at work |
| Dan's company | A company led by Dan and supported by a visible cast of practitioners |
| Interesting writing | Ideas, apps, and training people can use now |
| A collection of products | One company that helps people work with AI |
| A best-kept secret | A recognized authority on AI and work |

## Audience movement

- **Builders and Operators:** deepen affinity, badge value, and paid conversion through
  credible products, workflows, ideas, and a creative and technical bar they respect.
- **Seekers:** expand awareness and free subscriptions by making Every the clearest
  gateway into AI-native work.
- **Executives:** establish authority and create Consulting demand through Dan, original
  research, business press, events, public expertise, and client proof.

## End state

By the end of H1 2027:

- "Every makes you AI native" is the company's clearest public association.
- Builders and Operators show stronger affinity and paid conversion.
- More Seekers discover Every and become free subscribers.
- More Executives encounter Every through public authority and Consulting proof.
- Marketing has four quarters of comparable acquisition, cohort, fame, authority, and
  business-demand results.
- Growth has a larger, better-understood audience to convert.
