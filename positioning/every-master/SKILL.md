# Every Master Positioning

Positioning framework for Every as the parent brand. Every is an applied AI company
combining ideas, apps, training, research, and Consulting to help people and organizations
become AI native.

## Working position

**You want to build or work with AI. Every makes you AI native.**

Marketing establishes that association across Editorial, Studio, Training, Consulting,
products, launches, campaigns, events, and public communications. Every is a badge brand:
useful enough to earn trust, distinctive enough to signal taste and membership, and
consistent enough to compound.

Load `references/positioning-year-2026-27.md` for the current company-level positioning
agenda and `references/marketing-portfolio-2026-27.md` for the current program portfolio.

## When to invoke

- When defining or refining Every's master brand position
- When a strategy skill needs the parent-brand positioning context
- When evaluating how a sub-brand position relates to the master brand
- When building company-level campaigns, PR, research, events, or executive materials

## Quick checklist

- Does the work make Every easier to understand as one company?
- Does it strengthen the association "Every makes you AI native"?
- Does it adapt the promise to the intended audience without creating a separate brand?
- Does it preserve Every's premium, editorial, technically credible badge value?
- Does it include a clear proof point from ideas, apps, training, research, or Consulting?
