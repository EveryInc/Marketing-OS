# One-Pager Formats

## Executive format

Use for leadership, board, or company-wide review:

1. Decision or mandate.
2. Current gap.
3. Strategy and priority programs.
4. Measurement.
5. Ownership and asks.
6. Directional budget.
7. Next decision.

Design for a 10- to 15-minute discussion. Use the word "Owner," not RACI language, unless
the organization explicitly requests RACI.

## Team format

Use for internal alignment:

1. Objective and audience.
2. What the team is making.
3. Message and creative standard.
4. Workstreams, owners, and dependencies.
5. Timeline and approval points.
6. Measurement and postmortem.

## External format

Use for partners, press, or clients:

1. Every's point of view.
2. The audience or market problem.
3. The idea or offer.
4. Credible proof.
5. The specific partnership or next step.

Remove internal economics, unresolved debate, and organizational detail. Preserve the
idea's specificity.
