# Equity Phase

Brand equity modeling follows character and precedes identity execution. It is optional
for an early-stage or time-constrained brand, but the human must understand what is being
deferred.

Load `marketing-science/brand-equity` and use its Aaker-led system.

## Minimum useful model

- Core and extended identity
- Brand-as-product, organization, person, and symbol
- Brand essence
- Current and desired associations
- Distinctive assets and semiotic meaning
- Awareness, perceived quality, and loyalty hypotheses
- Audience-relative cognitive presence
- Cultural position and recuperation risk where relevant

## Human choice

Ask:

> Do you want to build the deeper equity model now, or save it until the brand has more
> customers and distinctive assets?

If deferred, record the missing information and a return trigger. Do not let a skipped
equity phase block verbal or visual identity when the strategic foundation is otherwise
approved.
