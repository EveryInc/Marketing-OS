# New Product Launch

Orchestrates a full launch for a net-new product — the heaviest launch tier.

## When to invoke

- When launching an entirely new product or sub-brand
- When the launch requires the full suite of channels and maximum coordination
- When introducing a product that needs fresh positioning and messaging from scratch

## Dependencies

- `foundation/marketing-os`
- `positioning/{relevant product}` (loaded dynamically)
- `brand-voice/{relevant product}` (loaded dynamically)
- `strategy/messaging-architecture`
- `craft/launch-email`
- `craft/linkedin-post`
- `craft/x-post`
- `craft/website-copy`
- `craft/press-comms`
