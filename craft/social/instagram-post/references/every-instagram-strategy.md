# Every Instagram Strategy

Instagram becomes Every's visual front door for Seekers while reinforcing technical and
creative badge value among Builders and Operators.

## Editorial model

Borrow selectively:

- Puck's taste and identifiable editorial personalities.
- Semafor's recognizable anchors.
- Fast AI accounts' speed.
- Runway's visual experimentation.

Every adds stronger editorial judgment and a clearer path from discovery to becoming AI
native.

## Content franchises

### The Edge

Fast visual coverage of AI news, releases, experiments, and culture, with Every's view
of why each development matters.

### AI Native in Practice

Workflows, product demonstrations, and practitioners working differently with AI. At
least half of the examples feature people outside Every.

### Every Digest

Articles, reports, podcast moments, products, and launches translated into carousels,
Stories, and short-form video.

### The Art of AI

Brand analysis, design and process dumps, visual inspiration, and collaborations with
artists and companies such as Midjourney, Flora, and Runway.

## Pilot cadence

Publish one face-led video, one editorial carousel, and supporting Stories each week.
Use unpaid collaborator posts from Every's network to accelerate discovery. Increase
cadence only after production is sustainable and the subscriber cohort is useful.

## Handle strategy

- `@every` is ideal. Pursue it only through a genuine, voluntary collaboration with
  artist Leif Gann-Matzen and legal review. The collaboration could create its own design
  or business press story.
- `@everyai` is the strongest backup. Petition Meta through legitimate business-support
  and trademark channels; inactivity does not guarantee release.
- `@everydotto` is the launch fallback. Reserve it so negotiations do not delay the
  pilot.

## Measurement

Primary:

- Attributed free subscribers.
- Direct and fully loaded CAC.
- Free-to-paid conversion, retention, and LTV:CAC over time.

Diagnostic:

- Non-follower reach.
- Video completion.
- Saves and shares.
- Story clicks.
- Follower growth.
- Branded search.
- Fame Score movement.
