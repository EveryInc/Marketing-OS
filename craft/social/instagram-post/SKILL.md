# Social — Instagram Post

Builds and executes Every's Instagram editorial strategy across Reels, carousels,
Stories, captions, collaborations, and launch moments. The account turns visual discovery
into free subscriptions without becoming a promotional feed.

## When to invoke

- When drafting Instagram captions for a launch, brand moment, or content series
- When a launch skill orchestrates channel execution and needs the Instagram deliverable
- When writing copy for carousels, Reels overlays, or Stories
- When planning the Instagram channel, content franchises, publishing cadence,
  collaborator strategy, or acquisition pilot

## Every channel promise

**Follow Every to stay at the edge of AI. Subscribe to become AI native.**

Every runs an editorial account. One recurring on-camera anchor leads the first six
months. Dan launches the account and appears at major moments; employees and outside
practitioners join as beat-specific experts.

Load `references/every-instagram-strategy.md` for content franchises, format roles,
collaborations, handles, and measurement.

## Channel-specific notes

- Visual-first platform — copy supports the image, not the other way around
- Reels drive discovery, carousels earn saves, Stories build habit, and collaborator
  posts bring established audiences
- Every editorializes AI in real time and translates its own work into visual-native
  formats
- The subscriber path and production cost are part of the deliverable

## Quick checklist

- Does the post make a Seeker feel closer to the edge of AI?
- Is there editorial judgment rather than promotional repackaging?
- Is the format native to Reels, carousels, or Stories?
- Does the visual idea carry the post before the caption?
- Is the collaborator genuinely relevant?
- Is the free-subscriber path tracked?
