# Press Comms

Builds Every's press strategy and writes the materials required to earn coverage. Press
combines an always-on expert-commentary desk with concentrated tentpole launches.

## When to invoke

- When pitching a journalist or outlet on a product launch or story
- When a launch skill orchestrates channel execution and needs the press deliverable
- When drafting a standalone PR pitch for a specific media target
- When building a press plan, spokesperson strategy, exclusive, press kit, or quarterly
  PR readout

## Operating model

### Always-on press

Maintain a steady cadence of timely expert commentary, relationship building, and
briefings. The operating target is at least one meaningful expert feature each month.
Dan is the lead public voice; the wider roster expands the number of credible stories
Every can enter.

### Tentpole press

Reserve the full launch machine for news that can change how the market sees Every.
Choose one major outlet and reporter whose audience and beat fit the story. Offer a
time-bound exclusive with early access, a private demonstration, the relevant
spokesperson, and the strongest evidence Every can share. Once the exclusive publishes,
release Every's definitive launch story and open distinct angles to the broader press
list.

## Channel-specific notes

- Every pitch is tailored to a specific journalist and outlet — no blast templates
- The angle must match the outlet's editorial lens rather than repeat the company's
  messaging
- Subject line and opening sentence determine whether the pitch gets read
- Impressions describe potential exposure; they do not prove impact
- Every does not pay media companies for coverage
- Paid partnerships are acceptable when the partner pays Every, the commercial
  relationship is clear, and Every retains editorial and creative control

## Quick checklist

- Is the story genuinely newsworthy for this reporter's audience?
- Does the pitch lead with the story rather than company background?
- Is the spokesperson matched to the subject?
- Are claims, methodology, screenshots, and proof ready?
- Is the exclusive narrow enough to preserve wider launch angles?
- Does the plan include authority, attention, acquisition, and business measurement?
