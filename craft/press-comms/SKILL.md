# Press Comms

Generates PR pitch emails based on product details, target outlet/journalist, and angle — crafted to land coverage.

## When to invoke

- When pitching a journalist or outlet on a product launch or story
- When a launch skill orchestrates channel execution and needs the press deliverable
- When drafting a standalone PR pitch for a specific media target

## Dependencies

- `foundation/marketing-os`
- `brand-voice/{relevant brand}` (loaded dynamically)
- `positioning/{relevant brand}` (loaded dynamically)
- `launches/{parent launch skill}` (when invoked inside a launch)
