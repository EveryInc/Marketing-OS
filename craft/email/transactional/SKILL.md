# Email — Transactional

Writes transactional emails — receipts, confirmations, onboarding, and system notifications that still carry brand voice.

## When to invoke

- When writing or rewriting transactional emails (signup confirmations, receipts, etc.)
- When onboarding sequences need brand-voice alignment
- When system-triggered emails need to feel human, not automated

## Dependencies

- `foundation/marketing-os`
- `brand-voice/{relevant brand}` (loaded dynamically)
- `positioning/{relevant brand}` (loaded dynamically)
- `craft/copywriting`
- `craft/editing`

Brand is resolved dynamically based on what invokes this skill.

## Channel-specific notes

- Highest open rates of any email type — treat as brand real estate
- Clarity and utility come first; brand expression is secondary but present
- Legal and compliance requirements may constrain format and content
